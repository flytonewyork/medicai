# Bridge Strategy Documentation

## Context as of April 2026

- RASolute 302 readout (April 13, 2026): daraxonrasib mOS 13.2 mo vs 6.7 mo
  chemotherapy, HR 0.40, p<0.0001
- RASolute 303 (1L) began enrolling April 2, 2026 — three-arm trial
- TGA/PBS pathway pending, expanded access anticipated

## RASolute 303

- Trial ID: NCT07491445
- Design: 1:1:1 randomisation
  - Arm A: daraxonrasib 300 mg PO daily (monotherapy)
  - Arm B: daraxonrasib 200 mg PO daily + GnP Q2W
  - Arm C: standard GnP weekly (control)
- Eligibility: confirmed metastatic PDAC, no prior systemic therapy for mPDAC,
  ECOG 0–1, RAS status for stratification
- **Time-sensitive**: first GnP dose = permanent ineligibility for the 1L window

## RASolute 302

- Trial ID: NCT06625320
- Design: daraxonrasib 300 mg vs investigator's choice chemo (GnP, mFFX,
  NALIRIFOX, FOLFOX)
- Eligibility: 1 prior line of 5-FU or gemcitabine-based regimen, ECOG 0–1,
  documented RAS status (G12/G13/Q61)
- Enrollment closing June 2026

## Australian access pathway priority order

1. RASolute 303 (if any Australian site active, treatment-naive window)
2. RASolute 302 at progression (if still enrolling, ECOG 0–1 preserved)
3. Compassionate / expanded access programmes (monitor Revolution Medicines
   announcements)
4. TGA approval (post-FDA, 6–12 months typically)
5. PBS listing (post-TGA, 6–12 months typically)

## Molecular profile requirements

- Germline: BRCA1/2, PALB2, ATM
- Somatic NGS: KRAS (G12D, G12V, G12R most common in PDAC), NRAS, HRAS,
  MSI, dMMR, BRAF, NTRK, NRG1
- Testing platforms: Foundation One, Tempus, or Australian equivalent
- ctDNA monitoring for progression detection (Signatera, Natera, Guardant)

## Bridge status scoring

The platform computes a composite "bridge status" score weekly:
- **Function score (0–100):** aggregate of PS, weight trend, grip, gait, practice
- **Disease score (0–100):** CA19-9 trend, imaging stability, ctDNA dynamics
- **Eligibility score (binary per trial):** meets criteria or not
- **Composite bridge score:** weighted combination, with function weighted
  highest (function preserved → options preserved)
